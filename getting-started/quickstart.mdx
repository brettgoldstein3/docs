# Quickstart

Get up and running with Micro in just a few minutes. Follow these steps to set up your workspace and start being productive.

## 1. Sign In

:::note Private Beta
Micro is currently in private beta. You will need to be whitelisted to access the platform.
:::

Visit [micro.so](https://micro.so) and sign in with your Google account. If you haven't been whitelisted yet, you'll see a message indicating that you need to request access.

## 2. Connect Your Accounts

After logging in, you can connect additional accounts to centralize all your communications:

- **Gmail accounts** - Connect multiple Gmail accounts to manage all your emails in one place
- **LinkedIn** - Sync your LinkedIn connections and messages
- **Twitter** - Connect your Twitter account for social media management
- **Team members** - Invite team members to share contacts with everyone

:::tip
The more accounts you connect, the more comprehensive your unified workspace becomes. You can always add more accounts later.
:::

## 3. Create an App

### Option A: Onboarding Flow
Follow the guided onboarding process when you first sign in to create your first app.

### Option B: Quick Setup
If you skip the onboarding, you can create an app anytime:

1. Press `Cmd+K` (or `Ctrl+K` on Windows) to open the command palette
2. Type "new app" and select it
3. Choose a template that fits your needs
4. You're all set up!

### Adding Records
Once your app is created, you can:
- **Add records manually** - Create new contacts, companies, or deals one by one
- **Import from CSV** - Bulk import your existing data using our [import tool](/getting-started/importing-migration)

:::success
That's it! You're ready to start using Micro. Explore the [feature guides](/using-micro/) to learn more about specific capabilities.
:::
