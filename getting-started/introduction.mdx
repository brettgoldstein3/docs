# Welcome to Micro

Micro is your all-in-one productivity platform that brings together email, messaging (LinkedIn and WhatsApp), calendar, docs, tasks, and CRM in one place. Everything is intelligently organized with people/companies enriched, records automatically updated, and an assistant to answer all your questions.
