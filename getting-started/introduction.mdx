# Welcome to Micro

Micro is your all-in-one productivity platform that brings together email, messaging (LinkedIn and WhatsApp), calendar, docs, tasks, and CRM in one place. Everything is intelligently organized with people/companies enriched, records automatically updated, and an assistant to answer all your questions.

## What is Micro?

**All-in-one** - Email, messaging (LinkedIn and WhatsApp), calendar, docs, tasks, and CRM in one place

**Intelligently organized** - People/companies enriched, records automatically updated, and an assistant to answer all your questions

## Key Features

### 🚀 **Smart Email Management**
Organize your inbox with AI-powered categorization, smart filters, and automated responses.

### 📅 **Intelligent Calendar**
Schedule meetings, set reminders, and manage your time with smart calendar insights.

### 👥 **People & Companies**
Build and maintain relationships with integrated CRM capabilities and contact management.

### 🤖 **AI Automations**
Automate repetitive tasks and workflows with intelligent AI assistance.

### 🔍 **Powerful Search**
Find anything instantly with universal search across all your data and communications.

### 📱 **Mobile & Chrome Apps**
Stay productive on the go with dedicated mobile and browser applications.
